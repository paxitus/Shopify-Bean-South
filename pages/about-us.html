The Story Behind the Bag


Bean South Coffee grew from a simple but powerful idea: that great coffee should be honest. Honest about where it comes from, who grew it, and how it got to your cup.

We are a project of Café Campesino, Georgia’s first and only 100% fair trade and organic certified coffee roaster. For over twenty-five years, we have been roasting specialty coffee right here in Americus, Georgia, building direct relationships with the small-scale farmers who grow the extraordinary beans that fill every bag we sell.

Bean South is where both Souths meet. The American South, with its deep-rooted traditions of hospitality, hard work, and community, and the Global South, where our partner farmers work from sun up to sun down to craft something truly remarkable. This coffee is the fruit of that shared project. We are all connected by the beans in this bag.

Americus, Georgia


We are headquartered in the historic heart of southwest Georgia. Americus sits just eight miles from Plains (hometown of President Jimmy Carter), surrounded by pecan groves, cotton rows, and peanut farms that stretch to the horizon.

This place shaped us. The warmth of a Southern community, the respect for honest work, the pride in doing things right. That spirit goes into every roast we make. When you brew a cup of Bean South Coffee, a piece of Georgia is in that cup.
