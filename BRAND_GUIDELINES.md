## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
- **Black version** on light backgrounds (primary use)

### Alternate Versions
- **White logo** on dark or tan backgrounds
- **Green logo** on dark or tan backgrounds

### Usage Rules

✅ **DO:**
- Maintain clear space around the logo for visibility
- Use on clean, solid-color backgrounds
- Ensure sufficient contrast for readability
- Scale proportionally

❌ **DON'T:**
- Stretch or distort the logo
- Recolor the logo beyond approved versions
- Apply effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
- Place over busy or textured backgrounds
- Use low-resolution versions

### Clear Space
Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the letter "B" in "Bean" on all sides.

### Minimum Size
- **Web:** 120px width minimum
- **Print:** 1 inch width minimum

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors

#### Coffee Brown (Primary)
- **Hex:** `#4A3728` (Dark roasted coffee brown)
- **RGB:** 74, 55, 40
- **Usage:** Headers, primary text, logo

#### Tan / Burlap (Background)
- **Hex:** `#E8DCC4` (Warm tan, burlap-inspired)
- **RGB:** 232, 220, 196
- **Usage:** Backgrounds, cards, sections

#### Forest Green (Accent)
- **Hex:** `#2D5016` (Rich forest green)
- **RGB:** 45, 80, 22
- **Usage:** Buttons, highlights, calls-to-action, links

### Secondary Colors

#### Cream / Off-White
- **Hex:** `#F5F1E8` (Soft cream)
- **RGB:** 245, 241, 232
- **Usage:** Alternate backgrounds, cards

#### Deep Red (Accent)
- **Hex:** `#8B2E2E` (Coffee berry red)
- **RGB:** 139, 46, 46
- **Usage:** Sale prices, urgency elements, special highlights

#### Black
- **Hex:** `#1A1A1A` (Rich black, not pure black)
- **RGB:** 26, 26, 26
- **Usage:** Body text, primary logo

#### White
- **Hex:** `#FFFFFF`
- **RGB:** 255, 255, 255
- **Usage:** Text on dark backgrounds, negative space

### Color Usage Guidelines

**Backgrounds:**
- Primary: Tan (`#E8DCC4`) or Cream (`#F5F1E8`)
- Sections: Alternate between tan and cream for visual hierarchy
- Dark sections (sparingly): Coffee Brown (`#4A3728`)

**Text:**
- Body text: Black (`#1A1A1A`) on light backgrounds
- Headings: Coffee Brown (`#4A3728`)
- Inverse: White on dark backgrounds

**Interactive Elements:**
- Primary CTA: Forest Green (`#2D5016`)
- Secondary CTA: Coffee Brown (`#4A3728`)
- Links: Forest Green (`#2D5016`)
- Sale/Urgent: Deep Red (`#8B2E2E`)

**Accessibility Note:** All color combinations must meet WCAG 2.2 Level AA contrast requirements (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text).

## Typography

### Primary Brand Font (Print)
**Cocogoose Pro – Letterpress**
- Bold, confident letterforms
- Used in print materials, packaging, and logos

### Web Font Stack

Since Cocogoose Pro may not be available or suitable for web, use these alternatives:

#### Headings Font
**Primary:** Montserrat (Google Fonts)
**Alternatives:** League Spartan, Poppins

```css
font-family: 'Montserrat', 'League Spartan', 'Poppins', sans-serif;
```

**Weights Available:**
- Bold (700) - Headlines, main CTAs
- SemiBold (600) - Subheadings
- Medium (500) - Emphasis

#### Body Font
**Primary:** Montserrat or Poppins (Google Fonts)

```css
font-family: 'Poppins', 'Montserrat', -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, sans-serif;
```

**Weights Available:**
- Regular (400) - Body text
- Medium (500) - Emphasized text
- SemiBold (600) - Strong emphasis

### Typography Guidelines

#### Headlines (H1, H2)
- **Font:** Montserrat Bold (700)
- **Style:** ALL CAPS or Title Case
- **Letter Spacing:** +0.05em for all-caps
- **Color:** Coffee Brown (`#4A3728`)
- **Tone:** Strong, confident, bold

#### Subheadings (H3, H4)
- **Font:** Montserrat SemiBold (600)
- **Style:** Title Case
- **Color:** Coffee Brown (`#4A3728`) or Forest Green (`#2D5016`)

#### Body Text
- **Font:** Poppins Regular (400) or Montserrat Regular (400)
- **Color:** Black (`#1A1A1A`)
- **Line Height:** 1.6-1.8 for readability
- **Size:** 16px minimum (1rem)

#### Buttons & CTAs
- **Font:** Montserrat Bold (700)
- **Style:** ALL CAPS
- **Letter Spacing:** +0.1em
- **Size:** 14px-16px

### Font Sizing Scale

```
H1: 2.5rem - 3rem (40px - 48px)
H2: 2rem - 2.5rem (32px - 40px)
H3: 1.5rem - 1.75rem (24px - 28px)
H4: 1.25rem - 1.5rem (20px - 24px)
Body: 1rem (16px)
Small: 0.875rem (14px)
```

**Responsive:** Scale down by 15-20% on mobile devices.

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice Characteristics
**Confident | Warm | Genuine**

The Bean South voice reflects:
- Southern hospitality and charm
- Pride in craftsmanship and quality
- Authenticity and transparency
- Community connection

### Tone
**Southern charm meets modern craftsmanship**

- Approachable yet professional
- Friendly without being overly casual
- Knowledgeable without being pretentious
- Warm without being saccharine

### Messaging Style

#### Emphasize:
- **Freshness:** "Fresh roasted daily," "Roasted to order"
- **Local Roots:** "Americus, Georgia," "Southern tradition"
- **Artisanal Process:** "Handcrafted," "Small batch," "Carefully selected"
- **Quality:** "Premium beans," "Expert roasting"
- **Community:** "Family-owned," "Locally roasted," "Community coffee"

#### Language Guidelines

**DO Use:**
- "Freshly roasted"
- "Handcrafted in small batches"
- "Rooted in Southern tradition"
- "Quality you can taste"
- "From our roastery to your cup"
- "Americus proud"

**AVOID:**
- Corporate jargon
- Overly technical coffee terminology (unless educational)
- Pretentious language
- Generic coffee clichés
- Slang or overly casual language

#### Example Copy Styles

**Homepage Hero:**
> "Fresh Roasted Coffee from the Heart of Georgia"
> "Quality beans, expertly roasted in Americus, Georgia"

**Product Descriptions:**
> "Rich, smooth, and full-bodied, our House Blend brings together the finest beans, roasted fresh daily in our Americus roastery."

**About Section:**
> "At Bean South Coffee, we believe great coffee starts with great beans and careful attention to the craft of roasting. Every batch is roasted by hand in Americus, Georgia, ensuring the freshest cup possible."

**Call-to-Actions:**
- "Shop Fresh Roasted Coffee"
- "Discover Our Roasts"
- "Taste the Difference"
- "Order Fresh Coffee"

## Visual Style

### Photography Style

#### Subject Matter
- Coffee brewing (pour-over, French press, espresso)
- Coffee beans (whole and ground)
- Community gatherings and coffee moments
- Southern landscapes (Americus, Georgia settings)
- Roasting process and behind-the-scenes
- People enjoying coffee in authentic settings

#### Photography Guidelines
- **Lighting:** Natural, warm lighting
- **Tones:** Warm, inviting color palette
- **Style:** Authentic, documentary-style moments
- **Composition:** Clean, uncluttered
- **Mood:** Welcoming, genuine, crafted

**AVOID:**
- Overly staged or artificial-looking photos
- Cold, harsh lighting
- Generic stock photography look
- Overly filtered or processed images

### Design Elements

#### Textures (Subtle Use)
- Burlap texture (subtle background)
- Wood grain (for sections/dividers)
- Coffee bean patterns (minimal use)
- Paper texture

**Usage:** Keep textures subtle and non-distracting. Never compromise text readability.

#### Layout Principles
- **White Space:** Generous padding and margins
- **Hierarchy:** Clear visual hierarchy with typography
- **Grid:** Clean, organized layouts
- **Balance:** Mix of text and imagery
- **Focus:** Guide eye to key information

#### Iconography
- **Style:** Simple, rustic, geometric
- **Weight:** Consistent line weight (2-3px)
- **Color:** Coffee Brown or Forest Green
- **Examples:** Coffee cup, coffee bean, location pin, clock (freshness)

### UI Elements

#### Buttons
- **Style:** Rounded corners (4-8px border-radius)
- **Primary Button:** Forest Green background, white text
- **Secondary Button:** Outlined Coffee Brown, transparent background
- **Hover State:** Darken by 10-15%

#### Cards
- **Background:** Cream or White
- **Border:** Subtle (1px solid tan) or shadow
- **Padding:** Generous (2-3rem)
- **Style:** Clean, minimal

#### Forms
- **Style:** Clean, simple
- **Border:** Tan or light gray
- **Focus State:** Forest Green border
- **Labels:** Clear, above inputs

## Web Implementation Guide

```css
:root {

  --color-coffee-brown: #4A3728;
  --color-tan: #E8DCC4;
  --color-forest-green: #2D5016;
  
  /* Secondary Colors */
  --color-cream: #F5F1E8;
  --color-deep-red: #8B2E2E;
  --color-black: #1A1A1A;
  --color-white: #FFFFFF;
  
  /* Semantic Colors */
  --color-primary: var(--color-forest-green);
  --color-secondary: var(--color-coffee-brown);
  --color-accent: var(--color-deep-red);
  --color-background: var(--color-tan);
  --color-background-alt: var(--color-cream);
  --color-text: var(--color-black);
  --color-text-light: var(--color-coffee-brown);
```

```css
/* Import Google Fonts */
@import url('https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Montserrat:wght@400;500;600;700&family=Poppins:wght@400;500;600&display=swap');

  --font-size-h2: clamp(1.75rem, 4vw, 2.5rem);
  --font-size-h3: clamp(1.5rem, 3vw, 1.75rem);

  font-family: var(--font-body);
  font-size: var(--font-size-body);
  line-height: 1.6;
  color: var(--color-text);
}
```

## Don'ts - What to Avoid

### Visual Don'ts
- ❌ Don't use neon or overly bright colors
- ❌ Don't use cold colors (blues, purples) as primary colors
- ❌ Don't use generic, overused stock photos
- ❌ Don't overcrowd layouts - maintain generous white space
- ❌ Don't use more than 3 font families
- ❌ Don't use overly decorative or script fonts for body text
- ❌ Don't apply heavy textures that compromise readability
- ❌ Don't use drop shadows on text (except for very subtle ones)

### Brand Voice Don'ts
- ❌ Don't use corporate jargon or buzzwords
- ❌ Don't be overly casual or use slang
- ❌ Don't make unsubstantiated claims
- ❌ Don't copy competitors' voice
- ❌ Don't be pretentious about coffee knowledge
- ❌ Don't use clichés ("Your daily grind," "Brew-tiful morning")

### Logo Don'ts
- ❌ Don't stretch or distort
- ❌ Don't change colors outside approved palette
- ❌ Don't add effects or filters
- ❌ Don't place on busy backgrounds
- ❌ Don't rotate or tilt
- ❌ Don't outline or add borders

## Brand Checklist

Use this checklist when creating new designs or content:

**Visual:**
- [ ] Colors from approved palette only
- [ ] Fonts: Montserrat for headings, Poppins for body
- [ ] Headlines in all-caps or title case
- [ ] Generous white space
- [ ] Natural, warm photography style
- [ ] Logo used correctly with clear space

**Content:**
- [ ] Emphasizes freshness and local roots
- [ ] Voice is confident, warm, and genuine
- [ ] Tone reflects Southern charm + modern craftsmanship
- [ ] Language is approachable and friendly
- [ ] No corporate jargon or clichés

**Accessibility:**
- [ ] Color contrast meets WCAG 2.2 AA standards
- [ ] Text is readable (16px minimum)
- [ ] All images have descriptive alt text
- [ ] Buttons clearly labeled

## Questions?

When in doubt, ask:
1. Does this feel authentic and handcrafted?
2. Does it reflect Southern warmth and quality?
3. Would this make someone want to support a local business?
4. Is it accessible and user-friendly?
5. Does it emphasize freshness and craftsmanship?

If the answer is yes to all five, you're on brand.
